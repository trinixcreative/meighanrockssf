What is your goal? Share Groups allow you to share records owned by Community users with other users, both internal and external – on their account. To create a Sharing Group, you must first create a Sharing Set, then inside the Sharing Set you have the Sharing Group sub-tab.
Scenario: you want to share cases created and owned by Community users with internal users and other external users on the same account. In that scenario you’d make a sharing set for your community, add cases to your sharing, then on the sub tab you can add any internal users then external users on that same account for members. In the group, ALL case records owned by community users are shared with those selected for members.
Note, Share groups functionality isn’t available to users with Customer Community Plus and Partner Community licenses.

Based on your question, I’d be happy to help with more info. Is it for all partners on all records? Is it for contacts on the same account? Are the partners the owners of the data? Do you have any lookup fields to account or contact or user? What license type?

Sharing Sets and account relationship sharing are quite robust…
