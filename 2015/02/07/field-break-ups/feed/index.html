There is one extra step I’d include if you have Salesforce integrated with any other system. Removing the field from the page layout, is fine, however before changing the Field Level Security talk with people who manage the other system(s) and have them check their system(s) to see if they are using the Salesforce field in question. Otherwise when you change the FLS, alarms may go off, and it could be in the middle of the night. Can lead to cranky co-workers.
